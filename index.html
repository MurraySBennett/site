About Me

PhD Candidate

My research examines individual cogntion and performance during tasks that require collaborative or competitive team work. I am currently conducting my research as a PhD student in the Newcastle Cognition Lab under the supervision of Ami Eidels and Scott Brown.

I also have an active interest in other team-based performance domains such as coaching hockey and exploring large sports data. I am waiting with fingers crossed for a non-Covid-disrupted wedding in November!
